About Eye Disease Detector

About the Project

The Eye Disease Detector is a deep learning-based web application that helps in identifying various eye diseases from retinal images. It can detect conditions like Cataracts, Diabetic Retinopathy, Glaucoma, and identify normal eyes.

This system is designed to assist medical professionals in preliminary diagnosis and to help individuals get early detection of potential eye problems.
