About Our System

Our Eye Disease Detection System uses cutting-edge artificial intelligence to help identify potential eye diseases from retinal images.

What We Detect

  • Normal Eyes: Healthy retinal images
  • Cataracts: Clouding of the eye's lens
  • Diabetic Retinopathy: Diabetes-related eye damage
  • Glaucoma: Optic nerve damage conditions

Technology

Our system is powered by:

  • Deep Convolutional Neural Networks
  • Trained on thousands of retinal images
  • High accuracy detection algorithms
  • Secure and private processing
Important Medical Disclaimer

This system is designed for educational and screening purposes only. It should not be used as a substitute for professional medical diagnosis. Always consult with qualified healthcare professionals for proper medical evaluation and treatment.
